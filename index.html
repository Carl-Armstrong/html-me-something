Tuberkinesis© 2019

When Dalton sought the Dark Powers, tuberkinesis hadn’t been his first choice. In fact, he hadn’t even known it was a choice. But the Powers granted whatever abilities they saw fit, so now, Dalton controlled potatoes with his mind.

It was a lot more useful than he had first expected. Flying potatoes weren’t the most powerful weapon, but they were plentiful, and Dalton had used them to overwhelm opponents and intimidate peons. But as useful as that was, Dalton made better use of his potatoes' eyes. He could spy on anyone within two miles, if they were near a potato.

Up to now, Dalton was, as one of the Powers had put it, “small potatoes,” but if things worked out today, that would change. He was preparing for a showdown with Fleur Gagne, a woman who had been granted her own ability by the Powers – the ability to grant intelligence to plants. Her ability bent to his will would be a major boost to his plans for domination. But first, he would need to get through her garden. Luckily for him, she had some potatoes.
